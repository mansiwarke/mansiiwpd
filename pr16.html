LOTUS

The lotus flower stands as a beacon of hope, purity, and spiritual awakening across various cultures. Renowned for its ability to emerge from muddy waters untainted and pristine, this aquatic bloom carries deep meaning and significance that has transcended time. It captivates botanists, spiritual seekers, and horticulturalists alike with its striking beauty, unique life cycle, and symbolic essence.

Sunflower

Sunflower, (genus Helianthus), genus of nearly 70 species of herbaceous plants of the aster family (Asteraceae). Sunflowers are native primarily to North and South America, and some species are cultivated as ornamentals for their spectacular size and flower heads and for their edible seeds. The Jerusalem artichoke (Helianthus tuberosus) is cultivated for its edible underground tubers.
